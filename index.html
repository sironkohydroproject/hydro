Strategic Location

Our micro hydro power plant is strategically positioned on the banks of River Sironko, at the foothills of the magnificent Mount Elgon. This prime location offers optimal conditions for sustainable hydroelectric generation.

The Sironko River, fed by Mount Elgon's abundant watershed, provides consistent water flow throughout the year. The natural elevation creates ideal conditions for our high-efficiency Pelton wheel turbine system.

Our commitment to environmental stewardship ensures that Mount Elgon's pristine ecosystem remains protected while we harness clean energy to transform local communities for generations to come.
